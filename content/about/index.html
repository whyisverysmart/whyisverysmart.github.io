About Me

I'm Huanyu Wang (王焕宇), a 4th-year undergrad at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, pursuing dual degrees in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics. I conduct research under Prof. Zhouhan Lin at the LUMIA Lab.

I am also an incoming Ph.D. student at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Beidi Chen at the InfiniAI Lab. My research lies at the intersection of Machine Learning Systems and High Performance Computing — I am especially interested in efficient LLM inference, KV cache compression, and decentralized serving systems.

Outside of academics, I enjoy basketball, Japanese anime, and traveling, and I also have a lovely cat!
